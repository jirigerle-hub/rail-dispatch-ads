## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR (EU Users Only)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

### 7.1 Right of Access (Article 15)
You have the right to request what personal data we hold about you.

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)
You can request corrections to inaccurate personal data.

### 7.3 Right to Erasure - "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)
You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions (legal obligations, legitimate interests).

### 7.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
You can request that we limit how we use your data.

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
You can request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

### 7.6 Right to Object (Article 21)
You can object to processing of your data for legitimate interests, including profiling for advertising purposes.

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
If we rely on your consent for any processing, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

### 7.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority:

- **Czech Republic:** Office for Personal Data Protection (ÚOOÚ) - https://www.uoou.cz/
- **Other EU Countries:** Find your DPA here: https://edpb.ec.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en

## 10. Your Choices & Opt-Out Options

- **Android Users:** Go to Settings → Google → Manage Your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings → Personalized Ads toggle

The most direct way to stop data collection is to uninstall the Application from your device.

### 10.3 Reset Your Advertising ID
- **Android:** Settings → Google → Manage Your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings → Reset Advertising ID
